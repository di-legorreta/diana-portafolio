ABOUT ME

Born under the bright pulse of Mexico City, I graduated from the Instituto Politécnico Nacional as a Broadcast Engineer with a specialization in Computer Science. For nearly two decades, I worked across Mexico’s major public and private broadcasters, from cultural networks to national TV leading the implementation of large-scale production and post-production systems that helped stories reach their audience.

Today, I live in Austria, where the mountains have replaced the skyline and every sunrise comes with a trail road. I start most mornings with a 10 km jog, and end them with the lovely company of my two children and my cat Dicke, they are the real bosses of this house. Lately, I’ve been having a wonderful time learning Front-End Development with SheCodes.
